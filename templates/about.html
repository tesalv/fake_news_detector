Fake News Detector

We are living in a new era of misinformation. Not only found in politics, but also in areas like vaccination, stock values and nutrition and it’s influencing elections, threatening democracy and fuelling extremism all around the world. We believe tackling this problem and identifying fake news before they spread is essential.



About the project

This Fake News Detector was created as a final project of the Data Analytics bootcamp in Ironhack. Since the 2016 U.S. presidential election, “fake news” has become a common term in the mainstream vernacular and it's impact of everone's lives in veing largely studied.With the rapid development of social networking services, each user in a social network has become both a spreader and receiver of information, and millions of people present, comment on, or share various topics on social media every day. Several studies show how fake news spread faster than real news in these means and how it affected elections, daily consumption and poloration of sociaty. Because of all this I believe that having a tool that help us to identify Fake News from Reliable News is crucial. This project was my attempt to fulfill this need with the hope of it being a first step to a future better informed and less polarised.
